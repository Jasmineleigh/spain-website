Parque del Retiro

The Parque del Retiro is a tourist attraction that occupies 350 acres of land. In this park, you may find individuals rowing boats along the lake, riding bikes, or relaxing in the gardens. In addtion to all of the available activities, you can find peacocks exploring the park with you.
